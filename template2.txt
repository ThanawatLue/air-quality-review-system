Appendix 4: Functional Specification and TestingOBJECTIVE This document is established to specify the functional specification and define the functional testing methodology for the Advanced Failure Monitoring System. The objective is to verify that the software system operates in accordance with the functional specification. It focuses on validating the system's outputs against provided inputs and predefined conditions.PROCEDUREExecute test scenarios to simulate the system’s functionality.Documentation: Record all functional outcomes in Attachment 7: Test Result to confirm the integrity of developed or enhanced features.Reporting: Summarize the testing status and current progress in Attachment 8: Test Progress ReportGENERAL DOCUMENTS3.1 Attachment 1: Signature List All personnel involved in the recording, reviewing, and verifying of testing documentation are required to provide their signatures in Attachment 1: Signature List.3.2 Attachment 2: Work Instruction Verification Specify the details and information of the Work Instructions (WI) or User Manuals associated with the testing process in Attachment 2: Work Instruction Verification.3.3 Protocol correctionThe procedures for managing Protocol Amendments or handling unexpected Deviations are as follows:In cases where a Protocol Amendment is required and identified prior to testing (Planned Action), or when relevant Work Instructions (WI) or User Manuals are updated without impacting the integrity of the test, such changes must be recorded in Attachment 3: Amendment Log & Report.In the event of an unplanned deviation, such as a deviation from procedure (e.g., missed testing steps) or a deviation from Acceptance Criteria/Expected Results, all findings must be documented in Attachment 4: Deviation Log & Report.3.4 Attachment 5: User Comments and Enhancement Requests Record Any recommendations, comments, or additional remarks from Validation/QA and Key Users/Super Users identified during testing (if any) shall be recorded in Attachment 5: User Comments and Enhancement Requests Record.3.5 Attachment 6: System Error Record In the event of system errors caused by Human Error, Infrastructure issues, or other factors, all findings must be documented in Attachment 6: System Error Record.3.6 Recording and Reporting of ResultsRecord the test results in Attachment 7: Test Result Sheet. To certify the outcomes, each page of the test result sheet must be signed at the bottom by the Tester and Reviewer. Furthermore, summarize the current status and progress of the testing in Attachment 8: Test Progress Report.RESPONSIBILITY[1]Actions Responsibilities Perform Testing  GPO-BPOReview Results GPO-BPO Head of section Head of Equipment Qualification section Approve Results GPO-BPO Director of Division TEST CASE SELECTION PROCEDUREThe test selection for Integration testing is based on a risk assessment.
